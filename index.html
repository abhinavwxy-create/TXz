Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join TXz Gaming?

You can apply through our "Join" section below. We look for players with high skill (KD > 5.0), competitive experience, and a positive mindset.

Which games do you compete in?

Currently, our primary focus is Garena Free Fire. We are planning to expand to other mobile esports like BGMI and COD Mobile in the near future.

Do you provide player salaries?

Yes, all our primary roster players receive competitive salaries, tournament winnings shares, and professional equipment support.
